About Boxes Flappy 2048

Hi! I'm a UCSD CSE Computer Engineering student, and this is my portfolio project for my HCI design class!

I love to code - my journey began with a simple drag-and-drop game design language back in junior high. My fascination with programming was born in that very summer camp. Coming from an environment where the answer to "Can I do that" was usually a resounding "no," I easily saw the possibilities programming provided. Ever since then, I have tinkered with programming everything from computer games to calculator programs to even this very website.

What makes me who I am? I, and many of my peers, would easily say that it's that I can't resist a challenge. Whether it be a programming competition or a Rubik's cube (Both of which I love), I strive to succeed, putting in as much effort as I possibly can. I can't replicate the rewarding feeling of completing a program in any other way.
