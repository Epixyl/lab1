Flappy 2048

A minigame combining the two famous games "Flappy Bird" and "2048".



This was a game I created on a whim in about 3-4 hours after a friend jokingly asked me what would happen if "Flappy Bird" and "2048" were combined together. In my interpretation, the bird becomes a chain of blocks representing the powers of two that combine together like the 2048 game. As you play, the chain becomes longer and longer, making it harder to weave between the gaps in the incoming walls, creating a difficulty curve innate in the design of the game.
